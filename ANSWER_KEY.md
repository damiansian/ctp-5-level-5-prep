# Test Prep Answer Key

## Verbal Reasoning

### Question 1
**Category:** Verbal Analogies  
**Subtheme:** Sequential/Temporal Relationships  
**Stem:** INTRODUCTION : CONCLUSION ::  
**Correct Answer:** A. sunrise : sunset  
**Incorrect Answers:**
- B. apartment : house
- C. bow : applause
- D. match : flame
**Reference:** Introduction and conclusion represent beginning and end, just as sunrise and sunset represent the beginning and end of a day.

### Question 2
**Category:** Verbal Analogies  
**Subtheme:** Antonyms/Opposing Concepts  
**Stem:** HOARD : DISPERSE ::  
**Correct Answer:** C. save : spend  
**Incorrect Answers:**
- A. obtain : acquire
- B. oppose : disapprove
- D. donate : support
**Reference:** Hoard and disperse are opposites (keeping vs. scattering), just as save and spend are opposites.

### Question 3
**Category:** Logical Reasoning  
**Subtheme:** Relative Ordering/Sequencing  
**Stem:** If Jessica is older than Sarah but younger than Nate, and if Rosa is younger than Sarah, which person is the oldest?  
**Correct Answer:** C. Nate  
**Incorrect Answers:**
- A. Jessica
- B. Rosa
- D. Sarah
**Reference:** Order from oldest to youngest: Nate > Jessica > Sarah > Rosa

## Vocabulary

### Question 1
**Category:** Word Meaning in Context  
**Subtheme:** Context Clues  
**Stimulus:** After resting for the winter, the bear emerged from the dark cave and headed toward the sunny meadow.  
**Stem:** What does the word emerged mean in the sentence?  
**Correct Answer:** C. came out  
**Incorrect Answers:**
- A. bent down
- B. turned around
- D. crossed over
**Reference:** Context shows the bear leaving its cave after winter, moving from inside to outside.
